Welcome to Mochi Mochi!

We're a fun little bakery that sells beloved Mochi Donuts! Mochi Donuts, also known as Pon de Rings, are a mix of a mochi and donut, giving it an extremely chewy softness! Originating from a fusion between American and Japanese culture, these things are a delight to eat!

They're so good that they'll make even this poor developer want some. Because, when there's a Mochi Donut stand just 10 mins away from your house, you'll just be begging to get some to top the day off!

But unfortunately, due to two weeks of non-stop coding, this developer was unable to buy some. And even when this man had time, there was a problem. Yes, that's right! The Mochi Mochi has...

Ever Changing Flavors!

YES!!! THAT'S RIGHT!! Everyday, we make three different flavors, everything ranging from Matcha Green Tea, to Chocolate, to Strawberry, to Doughnut Sugar, to Vanilla, and so much more! We even have Churro flavor!

And it is because of this neat little quirk, our poor developer has been unable to buy any of his favorite flavors. That is until TODAY! This Friday, one of the flavors is Boba Milk Tea, a great flavor for a Mochi Donut! Once this day is over, this developer will be picking up a dozen of these delectable treats.

Festival Season

During Festivals, you can catch us operating the Mochi Truck! Though we'll be happy to see you and happily give you some delicious Mochi Donuts, remember to be courteous!
